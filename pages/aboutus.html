Introduction

4 Elements is dedicated to teaching Myanmar language to international students who may not have had the opportunity to learn their mother tongue extensively. Our goal is to help you connect more deeply with Myanmar's culture and people through language learning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to teach Burmese to teenagers in Myanmar, especially international school students, who might have forgotten Burmese or are not proficient in it, and ensure that they improve their mother language skills.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to educate teenagers in Myanmar, particularly international school students, about the Burmese language and its importance.

Our History

In recent times, we have noticed that teenagers in Myanmar, especially international school students, are lacking proficiency in their native language, Burmese. Therefore, our group has taken the initiative to create a website called "4 Elements" to teach them the Burmese language.
